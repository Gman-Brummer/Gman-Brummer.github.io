Leaders & Civilizations

Explore some of the most iconic leaders in Civilization VI. Each leader brings unique strengths and strategies to the game. Choose wisely depending on your victory path!

Theodore Roosevelt (America)

Ability: Roosevelt Corollary — Bonus combat strength on home continent.

Best for: Cultural and Domination victories on large continents.

Cleopatra (Egypt)

Ability: Mediterranean's Bride — Trade routes give gold to both Egypt and the origin city.

Best for: Diplomatic and Cultural victories.

Gilgamesh (Sumeria)

Ability: Adventures with Enkidu — Can share pillaged rewards and levy city-state troops at reduced cost.

Best for: Early game domination and allied victories.

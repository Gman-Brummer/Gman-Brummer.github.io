Expansions & DLC

Civilization VI has received multiple expansions and downloadable content (DLC) packs that greatly expand gameplay, add leaders, and introduce new mechanics. Here's a breakdown of what's available and what each pack brings to the table.

Rise and Fall

  • Introduced Loyalty, Governors, and Era Score mechanics
  • 8 new civilizations and 9 new leaders
  • Enhanced city management and diplomacy features

Gathering Storm

  • Added environmental effects and climate change systems
  • New power and resource management systems
  • World Congress and Diplomatic Victory introduced

New Frontier Pass

  • 6 content packs released over a year
  • New game modes: Secret Societies, Heroes & Legends, and more
  • 9 new leaders and additional civilizations

Leader Pass

  • Expanded leader roster with alternate versions
  • Focus on giving players more diplomatic and strategic diversity
  • Additional game balancing and bug fixes

Which Should You Get?

If you're new, start with Gathering Storm for the most complete experience. The New Frontier Pass is great for players who want more variety and challenge. For veterans, the Leader Pass adds replay value with fresh takes on existing leaders.

Victory Strategies

There are many ways to win in Civilization VI. Here's a breakdown of the main victory types and how you can plan for each one.

Domination Victory

Goal: Capture the capital of every other civilization.

  • Focus on building a strong military early.
  • Prioritize technologies like Iron Working and Combustion.
  • Choose aggressive leaders like Gilgamesh or Alexander.

Science Victory

Goal: Launch a satellite, land a human on the moon, and establish a colony on Mars.

  • Build campuses and prioritize tech boosts.
  • Use internal trade routes to support your science cities.
  • Great scientists and spaceport placement are key.

Cultural Victory

Goal: Attract more visiting tourists than any other civilization's domestic tourists.

  • Build theater squares and focus on great works.
  • Open borders and trade routes help bring in tourists.
  • Use wonders and seaside resorts to boost appeal.

Religious Victory

Goal: Become the dominant religion in every civilization.

  • Found a religion early with strong beliefs.
  • Use missionaries and apostles strategically.
  • Defend your religion through theological combat.

Diplomatic Victory

Goal: Earn diplomatic victory points and win the World Leader vote.

  • Focus on diplomacy and city-state alliances.
  • Build diplomatic favor through envoys and resolutions.
  • Use aid requests and climate accords to gain favor.

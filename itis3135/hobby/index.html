What is a PC? Why Build One? How to Build

What is a PC?

A PC (Personal Computer) is a multi-purpose device used for gaming, work, and entertainment. It consists of essential components like a CPU, GPU, motherboard, and storage.

Why Build One?

Building a PC allows customization, better performance, and cost efficiency compared to pre-built systems.

How to Build

Assembling a PC involves selecting compatible parts, installing them into a case, and configuring the software for optimal performance.

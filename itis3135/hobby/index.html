What is a PC?

A PC (Personal Computer) is a multi-purpose device used for gaming, work, and entertainment. It consists of essential components like a CPU, GPU, motherboard, and storage.

Who Builds PCs?

PC building is popular among gamers, IT professionals, and DIY enthusiasts who want customized performance.

When is the Best Time to Build?

Prices drop during Black Friday, Cyber Monday, and back-to-school seasons.

Where to Buy PC Parts?

Parts are available at online retailers like Amazon, Newegg, and Micro Center.

How to Build a PC?

Assembling a PC involves selecting compatible parts, installing them into a case, and configuring the software.

Why Build a PC?

Customization, performance, and long-term value make PC building worthwhile.
